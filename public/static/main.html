Artist: Mastodon

Album: Emperor of Sand

Click: Metal

There is a lot of different opinions about Mastodon. I think there awesome, I'm 45 and grew up on metal and punk. By the time most bands make there fouth album the done, the magic is gone. These guys truly believe in what there doing, and it would seem like they really enjoy and care about one another. That chemistry allows them to keep producing music that keeps progressing. This is no different.

Artist: Air

Album: Talkie Walkie

Click: Electronica

Through the years I have heard Air on and off, but I never found, or heard anything that really hit home. Sometimes you just keep missing the mark. I was listening to some random songs on iTunes and Cheery Blossom Girl came on. Looked up the album it was off of, and that was it. This album is really good. It incorporates so many different elements of alternative and electronic styles and melds them into a really interseting listen.
